Mondstadt

Genshin Impact is one of the most fascinating game.

The City of Freedom is located in the northeast of the continent of Tivat. Between mountains and open plains, the dandelion-scented winds of freedom blow over Lake Sidre, bringing the blessings and grace of the wind god Barbatos to the city of Mondstadt, located on an island in the center of the lake.
